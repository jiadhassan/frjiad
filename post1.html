5 Common SEO Mistakes (and How to Fix Them)

by Jiad Hassan | ← Back to Blog

Published: July 2025

SEO can be incredibly powerful, but only when done right. Unfortunately, many businesses unintentionally sabotage their own rankings with simple mistakes. Here are the top 5 blunders I see over and over again—and how you can avoid them:

1. Ignoring On-Page Optimization

Many people skip basics like title tags, meta descriptions, proper headings, and image alt texts. These are small but mighty signals for search engines.

2. Keyword Stuffing

Adding too many keywords makes your content unreadable and spammy. Google is smart enough now to penalize it. Focus on natural usage.

3. Not Optimizing for Mobile

Over 60% of searches are mobile. If your site isn’t responsive or mobile-friendly, your rankings will suffer—no matter how good your content is.

4. Forgetting About Technical SEO

Slow-loading pages, broken links, poor internal structure—these can silently kill your visibility. Use tools like Google Search Console to audit regularly.

5. Neglecting Quality Backlinks

Backlinks from trusted sites are still one of the strongest ranking factors. Focus on getting featured in niche blogs, directories, or industry sites.

Remember—SEO is not about quick hacks. It’s a long-term game built on strategy, consistency, and empathy for your audience.
